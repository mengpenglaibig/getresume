adidas Originals Unite All Originals: Brand New TV Commercial Released—Eason Chan Collaborates with RUN DMC
阿迪达斯运动经典集结原创力量，发布全新电视广告，陈奕迅联手RUN DMC演绎原创精神
Today, two music icons go head to head in the ultimate culture clash when hip-hop legends Run DMC collide with the next God of Songs Eason Chan in the FW13 adidas Originals campaign. Sparks will fly as the true masters of collision Run DMC take on the next God of Songs Eason Chan in the studio to create a new contemporary anthem that will unite all originals. Right from the start, Run DMC has constantly collided with cultures, inspiring others at every turn. The rap pioneers were the first to unite hip-hop with rock and fashion with music. Now they’re at it again in a new-school tussle with the hottest mixmaster on the planet. The campaign TVC adopted a lot of high-tech and skills in its post-production, which presents unprecedented creative artworks to the audience. The campaign Eason Chan x Run DMC TVC will be launched in various TV channels tonight. You cannot miss it!
